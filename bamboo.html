Bamboo

For every 10 minutes you spend watching the stream, you receive 1 bamboo.
Bamboo can be used for entering giveaways, gambling, joining whitelisted servers, and redeeming awards. You can read more about bamboo here.

Bot Commands

!bamboo (Displays how much bamboo you currently have)
!gamble (amount) (If bot rolls 1-60: lose bet, 61-98: 2x bet, 99-100: 3x bet)
!info (Displays more info about how to earn bamboo)
!leaderboard (Links the leaderboard of the users with the most bamboo)
!rewards (Links the info about earning rewards with bamboo)
!time (Displays how much time you have spent in the stream)
!uptime (Displays how long Panda has been live for that stream)
